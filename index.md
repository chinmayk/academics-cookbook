# The Antidote

A cocktail for our troubled Coronavirus times. From my philosopher friend.

* 3 parts rye whiskey
* 1/2 part Anisette
* 1/2 part Triple-Sec
* 1/2 lemon
* 1 Tbsp maple syrup

Shake over ice. Garnish with lemon peel. 

_Note: it's somewhat sweeter than I'd like, and very boozy._
